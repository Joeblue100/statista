Top 100 Companies: Nigeria

Statista Toplist

Nigeria is the most populated country in West Africa. In 2019, its population stood at 200.9 million. In the same year, its real GDP per capita amounted to US$1,950.9, ranking it 116th in a global comparison. The unemployment rate in Nigeria has been increasing since 2010, amounting to 6.1% in 2019. The significant number of large companies in Nigeria is a strong indicator of its overall economic performance. Not only do these organizations provide jobs for the local market, but they are also able to achieve success in global markets, thus generating greater value. This Statista Toplist ranks the top 100 companies in Nigeria based on their revenue and includes household names such as MTN Nigeria Communications Plc and Zenith Bank Plc.
